About

Aromatic_Suprises.com . Every candle is hand poured with a high quality soy wax blend grown from North American farmers. We’ve chosen soy wax as it gives candles a cleaner and longer burn.Aromatic Suprises passionately captures the essence of creating scents designed to evoke memories and comfort. Working with top fragrance houses, we’ve created a unique blend of fragrances which will relax and rejuvenate.The Aromatic Suprises story began with a desire to create beautiful and natural candles with a memorable surprise. Life is about memories and we hope you will enjoy your candle.
